# 🌍 ECORA

### **Eco Cleanliness Observation & Real-time Analytics**

### AI-Powered Urban Environmental Intelligence Platform

---

## 🚨 Problem

Urban cleanliness management remains largely reactive.

Municipal authorities often rely on periodic inspections, manual surveys, and citizen complaints to identify waste accumulation, illegal dumping, and environmental degradation. This creates significant operational challenges:

* Delayed issue detection
* Inefficient resource allocation
* Lack of city-wide visibility
* Reactive cleanup operations
* Poor citizen engagement
* Limited predictive planning capabilities

As cities continue to grow, traditional waste-monitoring approaches struggle to scale effectively.

---

## 💡 Solution

**ECORA (Eco Cleanliness Observation & Real-time Analytics)** transforms urban cleanliness monitoring into an intelligent, AI-driven environmental intelligence system.

By combining computer vision, geospatial intelligence, predictive analytics, and citizen participation, ECORA enables cities to move from **reactive waste management** to **proactive environmental governance**.

The platform continuously converts environmental observations into actionable intelligence for both citizens and municipal authorities.

### MVP Link

https://ecorabyecorian.vercel.app/

# 🧠 Core Vision

ECORA is not just a waste detection tool.

It is an **Environmental Intelligence Platform** that follows the complete decision-making pipeline:

**Observe → Analyze → Predict → Act**

This allows city authorities to understand:

* What is happening
* Why it is happening
* Where it is happening
* What will happen next
* What action should be taken
